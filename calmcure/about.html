🌿 About CalmCure

CalmCure is a mental wellness platform designed to help users track emotions, reduce stress, and improve mental health through simple and calming tools.

🎯 Our Mission

To create a safe digital space where users can express emotions, relax their mind, and build healthy mental habits.

✨ Features

  • Mood Tracking
  • Private Journaling
  • Relaxation Exercises
  • Progress Tracking
